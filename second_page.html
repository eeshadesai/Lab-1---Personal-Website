Kathak

Kathak is my favorite hobby and I've been doing it for 9 years now.

I just did my graduation over the summer with four other girls

Kathak is the dance of storytelling and there are three main types

  • Jaipur
    • agreesive dance form
    • focuses on footwork
  • Banaras
    • focuses on circles from both left and right
  • Lucknow
    • focuses on smooth, graceful movements(this is the style I learned)

To learn more about the history of kathak, check out this website!

For my graduation, we had to perform ten dances, inculuding my solo, and we had a forty minute section with no music that we used to show the footwork and techniques that we learned over the years.
